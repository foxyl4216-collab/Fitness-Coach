Adaptive Fitness Coach is a mobile app that acts as a personalized digital fitness coach. Users define fitness goals and receive adaptive weekly workout and diet plans based on structured check-ins.

The app supports:

Fat loss coaching

Skinny muscle gain coaching

Belly fat focus track

Glute gain focus track

Manual + AI-assisted calorie tracking

The system adapts weekly using measurable inputs (weight, adherence, energy levels), creating a coaching loop similar to a human trainer.

The MVP focuses on safe, beginner-friendly fitness guidance without medical or visual body analysis.
